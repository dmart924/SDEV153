About Us

What is Mad Rokh Publications?

Created by artist Jacob Chartrand and writer Dan Martin to showcase quality, independent publications. We both have the desire to see entertainment make a return to form with quality storytelling. We endeavor to create good entertainment, and in addition to the current Remnants storyline we (Dan, Jake, or both) would also like to pursure other stories to be told, including

  • An expansion of the Remnants universe
  • A comic book horror anthology in vein of Creepshow
  • When Dan become proficient enough in coding, a video game
  • A novel

Jake does create artwork on commission. See the information below if you are interested.

We also use the following websites frequently as useful references when trying to create a good story.

  • Wikipedia
  • TV Tropes

Jacob Chartrand


Jake has been an avowed fan of comic books for his entire life. He has spent many years honing his skills as an artist, and has many quality works to show for it.

Dan Martin


Dan is a big fan of movies and has a lot of opinions on the movies he has seen. One of his most controversial opinions is that the extended cut of Lord of the Rings is inferior to the theatrical release. He also collects too many books.
