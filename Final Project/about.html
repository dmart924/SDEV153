About Us

What is Mad Rokh Publications

Created by artist Jacob Chartrand and writer Dan Martin to showcase quality, independent publications. We both have the desire to see entertainment make a return to form with quality storytelling. He does takes artwork on commission. See information below if you are interested.

Jacob Chartrand


Jake has been an avowed fan of comic books for his entire life. He has spent many years honing his skills has an artist, and has many quality works to show for it.

Dan Martin


Dan is a cinephile and has a lot of opinions on the movies he has seen. One of his most controversial opinions is that the extended cut of Lord of the Rings is inferior to the theatrical release.
