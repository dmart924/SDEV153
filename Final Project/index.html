Welcome to Mad Rokh

Mad Rokh Publications has been writing and self-publishing digital comics since 2019. We do our best to keep the wheels of creation turning, even in our down time.


We are home to Remnants, a digitally based sci-fi epic. Our first priority will remain Remnants, until we are far enough off the ground to be able to tackle multiple projects simulataneously. We both work fulltime in addition to work on the comic and other ventures that we need in order to live. It is important to us that we keep pushing, and especially that we retain our self-respect while doing so. We highly value our artistic autonomy and don't wish to give that up easily.
